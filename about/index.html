May Hathaway is a Chinese-American high school student from New York City. Her work is published or forthcoming in Hobart After Dark, PANK, and Vagabond City Lit and has been recognized by the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards and the National YoungArts Foundation. She is an alumna of the Adroit Journal Summer Mentorship Program and the Iowa Young Writers' Studio. May likes dark chocolate covered pretzels, Bury Me at Makeout Creek by Mitski, and long naps. She will attend the University of Pennsylvania in the fall.
